Mindustry. think fast.


PREPARE

Mindustry is a game all about preparation before the first wave of robots start knocking on your base.
Mine resources, build generators, place turrets and pray you know what you are doing,
because the game gets real complicated really fast.

DEFEND

Place your structures strategically, placing turrets too far may cause it to never activate due to range
while placing a turret too close to the predicted enemy path may cause them to be destroyed in seconds.

SURVIVE

Your core has very limited health and contains explosive material that is already in the verge of catching
The slightest enemy disturbance may cause you and your little core to have a unfortunate ending
